INTERESTING FACT

According to Nielsen data, in some cases, users
spend up to 45 minutes choosing something to
watch on streaming platforms.

This happens especially when more people are involved in the
decision, such as in a family or social setting. This can happen due to
the overwhelming number of options and the difficulty in reaching a
consensus among different tastes and preferences.

This overload of choices is referred to as the "paradox of choice" and
impacts the overall user experience.
